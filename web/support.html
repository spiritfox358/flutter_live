FAQ

How do I get help with login or account access?

Email support with your Coin Dance user ID, registered account, device model, and a screenshot of the issue.

What should I do if gifts, coins, or recharge records look wrong?

Send the order time, amount, App Store receipt details if available, and screenshots. We will review the purchase record.

How do I report live room audio, video, PK, or chat problems?

Include the room name or room ID, what happened, your app version, and whether the issue happened on Wi-Fi or mobile data.
